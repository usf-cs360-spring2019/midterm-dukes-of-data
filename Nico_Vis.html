Average Response Time per Month for Each Neighborhood in 2018

Interpretation

This is a multiple line graph that is depicting the Average Wait Time per Month for Each Neighborhood in 2018. Each line in the chart represents the average wait times for the fire department (in minutes) for that given neighborhood. When you hover over a line the neighborhood that it represents shows up on the chart and when you hover over a dot on the line the actual wait time is displayed.
